Rule:
・ Try to clear the game by avoiding obstacles and breaking through the score.
・ The score reflects the number of obstacles that disappear at the bottom of
　the screen.
・ There are four stages, and the difficulty level increases from Stage 01 to
　Stage 04.
How to play:
・ Enter Key: Start game
・ Left Key : moving to Left
・ Right Key: Moving to Right
Obstacle Characteristics:
・ Red: Nomal speed one. Sometimes get bigger
・ Green: Faster than red.
・ Orange: It bounce on the walls
